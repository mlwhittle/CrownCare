CrownCare Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2026

1. Health & Biometric Data

CrownCare allows users to upload photos of their hair and scalp for local tracking purposes. This data is securely stored within the Google Firebase architecture. We do not sell your personal health logs, photographs, or dietary tracking data to third-party data brokers.

2. Apple Health Integration

If you choose to sync your biological metrics via Apple Health, CrownCare strictly utilizes this read-only data (like sleep cycles) to provide tailored haircare insights. This data is processed securely and is never harvested for external advertising algorithms.

3. External B2B Connections

If you manually enter a Stylist Code to link your account to a professional hair care provider, your connected stylist will gain explicit read access to your visual diary to provide professional consulting. You may revoke this access at any time via the Settings menu.

4. User Data Deletion

You retain full right to manually expunge all photographic and textual data generated by your account. Navigate to Settings and select "Clear All Data" to physically purge your records from the active Firebase production database.
