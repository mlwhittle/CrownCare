# CrownCare Context & Memory Profile

## Brand Identity & Mission
*   **Name:** CrownCare
*   **Tagline:** The World's First Clinical AI Hair Coach for Black Women and 4C Textured Hair.
*   **Aesthetic & Vibe:** "Clinical Luxury" - Premium, highly scientific, empowering. It should feel like a high-end medical spa met an elite AI tech platform. Colors are typically rich Crown Golds, deep Primary background hues, with glassmorphism and subtle glows.
*   **The Founder's Story (Core Motivation):** "For years, I watched my wife silently struggle with severe hair loss and breakage. We tried thousands of dollars worth of 'magic' oils and heavy creams, and nothing worked. Her hairline was suffering, and it broke my heart. So, I built something that actually works. We threw out the guesswork and built CrownCare—the world's first Clinical AI Hair Coach specifically designed for Black women and 4C textured hair."

## The Problem
Most hair apps treat every head the same. Users with Type 4 hair (specifically 4C) suffer from unique issues like high/low porosity moisture retention problems, traction alopecia (edge loss) from protective styles, hormonal shedding, and severe breakage. They rely on guesswork and confusing internet advice, treating the outside instead of the root cause.

## The CrownCare Solution (Core App Features)
CrownCare relies on strictly data-driven tracking based on the Walker Hair Typing System.
1.  **AI Hair Coach:** Instant, clinical-grade analysis of hair health based on user porosity and inputs.
2.  **Visual Hair Diary:** An encrypted vault to take weekly photos of specific scalp zones (Crown, Edges, Part Line) for undeniable "Before & After" proof.
3.  **Nutrition Planner:** Tracks exact DHT-blocking vitamins, iron, and proteins that fuel follicle growth from the inside out.
4.  **Daily Routines & Treatments:** Prescribes exact LOC (Liquid-Oil-Cream) layering methods, warns against "Protein Overload," and tracks Minoxidil/PRP/Natural Oils usage.

## Technical & Legal Status
*   **Platform:** React/Vite progressive web app, utilizing Stripe for payments and Firebase/OpenAI for the backend intelligence. 
*   **IP Protection:** The UI, code, and name ("CrownCare") are prepared for US Copyright and Trademark filing under digital/software categories (Classes 009 & 044). The clinical logic flow (Visual Diary -> AI -> Nutrition Plan) is structured for a Provisional Patent.
